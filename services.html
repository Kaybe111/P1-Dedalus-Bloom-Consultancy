Our services at a glance

Operational Excellence

Smoothly running processes, optimised for cost efficiency, with a high level of quality and also operating to the satisfaction of management, line staff AND customers are not the stuff of fantasy, although many will say they are practically impossible to achieve. Not so!
Our team's bespoke ODISSEE methodology combines stress testing of inputs, workflows and outputs to deliver lean practices which are unique, tailoring each solution through mindset trainings and coachings to embed the drive for excellence into the culture of your people and your Board.

Transparent Performance

Business Intelligence has made a jump in recent years in terms of access to data, interconnectivity of sources and multi-level visualisation tools which are served to smartphones, desktops, tables and workfloor monitors. If your business hasn't yet made the leap into this new world you run the serious risk of losing some of your competitive edge. And even if you consider yourselves ahead of the pack, perhaps you find your people drowning in the data lake, or not being able to see the proverbial dashboard wood for the graphical trees. Either way, we can help.
Let us guide you through the decision making process to help you get the most out of the modern tools and solutions on the market. Insights only come with context, and we'll show you how to connect all areas of company performace to best allow you to steer the ship and keep your stakeholders smiling and your customers singing.

Omni-channel Integration and Infrastructure

How many ways can your customers reach you these days. We're willing to bet that it's a minimum of three, but did you know that contact channels now extend to perhaps 11 or even 13 different possibilites for you to engage with your clients. That's a lot of ways to make money, but for even an experienced organisation, management of these channels, designing and measuring the customer experience and ensuring operational competence is not always going to be easy. That's where we come in.
Our specially developed SYREN approach will support you in deciding what type of channel you really need to be effective based on your business model and the expectations of the modern customer, whether that be B2C or B2B interactions. You might already be always on, but we will help you to be always there when you're needed.

CX and Organisational Transformation

The last 15 years have seen a proliferation of fanatical dedication to customer wants, needs and desires at all costs and at all times. Perhaps you have a department dedicated to Customer Experience and you have unlocked the power of agile methodology and design thinking. Perhaps you need convincing. Or maybe you had your fingers burned and are wondering if it all is an effective way to spend budget. Good doubts to have in our opinion, and as with the customer, essential questions need to be asked before spending your hard-earned money on something that sounds too good to be true.
Design is the name of the game. Whatever your philosophy and experience with the CX industry, we are convinced of one thing for certain - companies who actually design the customer journey and not just the experience - are the players who will spend their money well and ensure maximum profitability. It doesn't have to be rocket science, but it can be. We know how to approach it and can help you craft not just a winning customer engagement strategy but also how to engineer an organisation that will deliver on the promise.
